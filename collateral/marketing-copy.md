## Writing rules (why these work)
- Lead with the PAIN in the reader's words (chasing fees, typing report cards, lost registers) — not with features.
- One concrete moment beats three adjectives: "receipt on WhatsApp the moment they pay" > "seamless communication".
- Always end with ONE action: the demo link or the WhatsApp number, never both competing.
- Never invent numbers of schools/users. When real testimonials exist, swap them in — a named school with a quote will outperform everything in this file.
